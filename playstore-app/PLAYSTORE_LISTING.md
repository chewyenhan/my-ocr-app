### 🔑 How It Works (IMPORTANT)

1. **Get a FREE Gemini API Key** from [Google AI Studio](https://aistudio.google.com/apikey) (takes 1 minute)
2. Enter the key in Snaptext Settings
3. **Take photos** or upload from gallery
4. Tap **"Extract All"** — AI extracts all text at once
5. **Export** as TXT, PDF, or copy to clipboard

> 💡 **Why bring your own API key?** You pay Google directly (free tier available for light use). Snaptext has NO recurring fees. **Buy once, use forever.**
