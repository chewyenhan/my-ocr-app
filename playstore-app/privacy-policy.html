Privacy Policy

Snaptext: Batch Photo OCR

Last updated: May 28, 2026

Data Collection and Usage

Snaptext is a client-side OCR tool. All processing happens on your device or through the Gemini API using your own API key. We do not operate any backend servers.

What data is processed?

  • Photos you capture or select — sent directly from your device to the Gemini API for text extraction. Photos are not stored by us.
  • API Key — stored locally on your device in browser localStorage. Never transmitted anywhere except to the Gemini API endpoint (generativelanguage.googleapis.com).
  • Extracted text — remains on your device. You may choose to export it as TXT or PDF.

Third-Party Services

This app uses the Google Gemini API. When you extract text, your photos are sent to Google's Gemini service. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for how they handle data.

Data Retention

We do not collect, store, or have access to any of your data. All photos, API keys, and extracted text remain exclusively on your device.

Permissions

  • Camera — used only when you choose to take a photo for OCR
  • Photo Library — used only when you choose to select existing photos for OCR
  • Storage — used only to save exported TXT and PDF files
